公司简介Company Profile

沧县庆琛金属制品有限公司，坐落于河北省沧州市沧县崔尔庄镇，是一家专业生产不锈钢内外丝产品及水暖管件的现代化企业。公司主营产品包括：不锈钢六角接头、六角外丝、内外丝接头、水管接头、弯头、三通、活接、管箍、补芯、管帽及其他配件。QingChen Metal Products Co., Ltd, located in Cangxian, Cangzhou, Hebei, is a modern enterprise specializing in stainless steel fittings including hex nipples, elbows, tees, unions, couplings, bushings, caps and other accessories.

产品严格按照 API、ANSI、BS、DIN、JIS 等国际标准设计生产，远销北美、南美、西欧、中东及东南亚市场。我们拥有多年的产品定制经验，能够满足不同客户的个性化需求。每一件产品出厂前均经过严格检验，确保品质达到最高标准。Products are designed and manufactured in accordance with international standards: API, ANSI, BS, DIN, JIS, exported to North America, South America, Western Europe, the Middle East and Southeast Asia. With years of experience in product customization, every item undergoes thorough inspection.
